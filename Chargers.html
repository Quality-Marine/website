Ratelco Retro-Fit Kit
 300-2600-KT Upgrade an older McCarron or Ratelco VM charger to Smart Charge. Features include:
Float/Equalize voltage adjustments, equalize time control, soft start, cross talk elimination,

and install instructions

 Ammeter and FL/EQ switch with equalize indicator light. Panel NOT included
VMI Battery Fail Safe Kit
 VMI BFS-KIT Battery fail safe compensation kit, included in all 2008+ models.
Protects the VMI control board and connected equipment from elevated AC ripple
due to dead batteries, cell shorts, and accidental battery disconnection.
Improved EMI properties. Recommended installation instructions included
