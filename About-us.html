Who We Are

Quality Marine Electronics opened their doors in 1995 providing commercial marine navigation and communication support all around the Pacific Northwest waterways.


Quality Marine acquired the VMI charger line from McCarron Corp in 2010; and brought on Mike McCarron as the lead engineer to continue, and evolve, the VMI chargers. Since the acqusition, the upgrades to the VMI chargers have included: all stainless steel hardware, all aluminum powder-coated enclosure, an all new custom heatsink, a new transformer design to improve efficiency and reduce operating temperatures, and a redesign of the VMI control PCB to optimize function.


In late 2013 Quality Marine acquried Quality Computer Consulting, and with it Ryan Watkins. With this acquistion, Quality Marine has been able to branch into Electronic Charting computer systems and is certified to support Transas and RosePoint ECS & ECDIS systems.
